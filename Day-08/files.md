"CHEAT MODE OF FILE-HANDLING"
Mode	 Meaning	         Description
"r"   	Read	         Open for reading (default)
"w"   	Write	         Open for writing, truncates file
"a"	    Append	       Open for writing, appends to end
"x" 	 Exclusive	     reate new file, fails if exists
"r+"	 Read+Write	      Open for reading and writing
"w+"	 Write+Read	  Open for reading and writing, truncates
"a+"	 Append+Read	  Open for reading and appending
